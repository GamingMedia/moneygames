Understanding RTP

What Does RTP Actually Mean For Your Wallet?

RTP is widely misunderstood. Here's what it actually means — and doesn't mean — for your session.

96%
Typical SA Market RTP
Most Pragmatic Play slots run at 96–96.5% RTP. For every R100 wagered over millions of spins, R96 returns to players on average. This is a statistical average — not a per-session guarantee.
98%
Habanero's Edge
Koi Gate at 98.02% RTP is one of the highest-RTP slots available at SA casinos. The 2% difference extends your session meaningfully — R2 extra per R100 wagered returned to you over time.
35x
Wagering Requirements
A 35x wagering requirement on a R1,000 bonus means you need to bet R35,000 before withdrawing bonus winnings. Higher RTP slots extend your bankroll during wagering — but check which slots count 100%.
Vol.
Volatility vs. RTP
RTP is long-term return. Volatility is short-term feel. Low volatility: frequent small wins. High volatility: long dry spells with potentially massive wins. Both can have identical RTPs.
